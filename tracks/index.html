Train Tracks¶

Dragon Railway uses 22.5mm tracks - S scale - Standard gauge

Types of tracks¶

1) Straight tracks

  • 50mm
  • 100mm
  • 150mm
  • 200mm
  • 250mm
  • 300mm

2) Curved tracks

  • Radius 300mm - 15°, 30°, 45°
  • Radius 350mm - 15°, 30°
  • Radius 400mm - 15°, 30°
  • Radius 500mm - 15°
  • Radius 600mm - 15°
  • Radius 800mm - 15°

3) Turnouts

  • Left - R400
  • Right - R400

4) Crossings

  • 90° crossing -100mm length
  • 30° crossing -100mm length
